## üéØ Adventure Synopsis

### The Hook
Your players are recruited by **Olp Zix Zap**, a sophisticated kobold crime boss, to prove themselves worthy of joining the **Kobold Klaws**, an interplanar criminal syndicate. Their test: steal the **Shard of Eternal Binding** from the heavily-guarded Twilight Vault auction house.

### Three-Phase Structure

#### **Phase 1: Infiltration** (30-45 minutes)
- Disguise as catering staff during a high-class auction
- Gather intel from drunk nobles and security lapses
- Optional: Raid the security office for the Shield Guardian's control amulet
- Navigate social encounters with paranoid security chief Vexxus

#### **Phase 2: The Vault** (45-60 minutes)
- Bypass a glowing arcane lock (password, magic, or skill)
- Navigate pressure-plate trapped hallway
- Deal with a Shield Guardian (combat or control amulet)
- Disable force field protecting the Shard
- Alarm triggers - 1 minute to escape!

#### **Phase 3: Rooftop Escape** (45-60 minutes)
- Chase sequence through the panicking auction house
- Rooftop wave defense (3 rounds)
- Face off against Vexxus the cambion and his guards
- Dive through Olp's portal to safety

### Rewards
- 1,000 gp per character
- 1 uncommon magic item (player choice)
- **Kobold Klaw Pin** (magical item granting underworld contacts)
- Full membership in the Kobold Klaws syndicate

## üé≠ Running the Adventure

### Session Zero Considerations
- Set expectations: This is a heist, not a dungeon crawl
- Encourage creative problem-solving
- Stealth and planning are rewarded
- Violence is expensive (literally - Olp dislikes messes)

### DM Tips
- **Reward Creativity:** If players devise a clever plan you didn't anticipate, let it work!
- **Ham Up Olp:** Make him memorable with refined mannerisms and quotable lines
- **Scale Difficulty:** Use the difficulty adjustment guidelines in the DM guide
- **Time Management:** Keep phases moving; don't let planning bog down play
- **Music Matters:** Use the suggested playlist to set the mood

### Scaling Options

**Easier (for inexperienced players or bad luck):**
- Remove 1 hell hound from rooftop
- Shield Guardian has 100 HP instead of 142
- Portal opens at end of Round 2 instead of Round 3
- One pressure plate is already disabled

**Harder (for experienced/optimized parties):**
- Add 2 more veteran guards
- Guardian regenerates faster and has magic resistance
- Vexxus uses Fiendish Charm more aggressively
- Portal takes until Round 4 to stabilize

## üêõ Troubleshooting & FAQ

### "My players bypassed the entire vault!"
**A:** Reward creativity! If they found a clever solution (Dimension Door, Etherealness, etc.), that's great! Move to the escape phase and have Vexxus arrive early because the alarm went off.

### "They killed Vexxus in Phase 1"
**A:** Unlikely but possible. This makes the Twilight Vault very angry with the Klaws. Olp is annoyed but impressed. Replace Vexxus in Phase 3 with an angrier substitute (maybe a pit fiend accountant?).

### "TPK in the vault"
**A:** The Shield Guardian knocks them out, not kills. Guards arrive and arrest them. This becomes a prison break one-shot! Olp is disappointed but offers a redemption job.

### "Players want to join a different syndicate"
**A:** Absolutely! Use the rival syndicate hooks. Maybe they play both sides? Double-agent campaign?

### "They want to recruit the gang members"
**A:** Perfect! That's what the gang member stat blocks are for. Let them build their own crew within the Klaws.
