# IRON SCALES PRISON BREAK - QUICK REFERENCE SHEET

## MISSION BRIEFING
**Client:** Wicket Copperspring (gnome seeking "revenge")
**Target:** Ravens Blackwood (imprisoned assassin, Cell D-7)
**Location:** Iron Scales Prison (maximum security facility)
**Payment:** 2,500 gp upfront + 2,500 gp on delivery + 1,000 gp bonus from Olp
**The Twist:** Wicket hired Ravens to kill his family. This is a payment, not revenge.

---

## QUICK DCs

### Social/Infiltration
- Bluff past checkpoint: **DC 15**
- Impersonate guard: **DC 17**
- Sneak past patrols: **DC 13-16**
- Distract guard: **DC 14**

### Prison Security
- Pick cell lock: **DC 18** (1 minute)
- Force cell bars: **DC 22 Str** (loud, 2 rounds)
- Disable alarm ward: **DC 18 Arcana**
- Climb prison walls: **DC 15** (30 feet)

### Chase Sequence
- Goal: **6 successes before 3 failures**
- Athletics: **DC 14**
- Acrobatics: **DC 13**
- Stealth: **DC 15**
- Deception: **DC 14**

---

## KEY NPCS

**Wicket Copperspring** (Client)
- AC 12, HP 22
- Cowardly gnome, killed his own family
- Will flee or beg if threatened
- Carries 5,000 gp payment

**Ravens Blackwood** (Target)
- AC 16 (12 in prison), HP 78
- Professional assassin, amoral
- Assassinate ability (advantage + crit on surprise)
- Sneak Attack +4d6
- Knows the truth about Wicket

**Warden Grimstone** (Boss)
- AC 18, HP 112
- Three longsword attacks +8 (1d8+4)
- Action Surge, Second Wind, Indomitable
- Never gives up pursuit

**Mage-Captain Elara Voss** (Boss)
- AC 15 (20 with Shield), HP 66
- Flies, casts Fireball/Counterspell
- 12th-level wizard
- Targets spellcasters first

---

## PRISON LAYOUT

### Key Locations
**Ground Floor:**
- Entry Hall (checkpoint, 2 guards)
- Warden's Office (evidence, keys)
- Guard Barracks (20 guards off-duty)
- Kitchen (supply deliveries)

**Second Floor:**
- Cell Blocks A-D (increasing security)
- **Cell Block D:** Maximum security
- **Cell D-7:** Ravens' cell (anti-magic field active)

**Basement:**
- The Vaults (prisoner belongings)
- Secret tunnel (collapsed, can be cleared)

**Exterior:**
- Bridge (main entrance, guarded)
- Docks (supply entrance, 1 guard)
- Walls (30 ft high, watchtowers)

---

## THREE APPROACHES

### 1. THE VISITOR (Social)
- Pose as lawyer/clergy visiting Ravens
- Forge credentials (Forgery Kit)
- Swap Ravens with disguised member
- Low violence, high deception

### 2. THE DELIVERY (Stealth)
- Infiltrate as supply workers
- Navigate to Cell Block D quietly
- Free Ravens, escape via boat
- Moderate risk, requires planning

### 3. THE RIOT (Chaos)
- Get arrested on purpose
- Start prison riot during meal time
- Use chaos to reach Cell Block D
- High risk, high distraction

---

## SECURITY MEASURES

**Physical:**
- 50 guards total (20 on duty)
- Patrols every 20 minutes
- Cell locks DC 18
- Steel bars AC 19, HP 50

**Magical:**
- Anti-magic field in Cell Block D (20 ft radius)
- Alarm wards on exterior doors
- Scrying sensors (Mage-Captain watches)

**Response Times:**
- First patrol: 2 rounds after alarm
- Warden: 5 rounds after alarm
- Mage-Captain: 5 rounds after alarm
- Full lockdown: 3 rounds after alarm

---

## COMBAT ENCOUNTERS

### Standard Guards
- AC 16, HP 27
- Spear +4 (1d6+2) or Crossbow +3 (1d10+1)
- Blow whistle if combat starts

### Veteran Guards
- AC 17, HP 58
- Two longsword attacks +5 (1d8+3)
- Disciplined, fight in formation

### Guard Dogs
- AC 12, HP 11, Speed 40 ft
- Bite +3 (1d6+1), knock prone DC 11
- Track by scent, trip targets

### Boss Fight (If Caught)
**Location:** The Courtyard
**Enemies:**
- Warden Grimstone (fighter)
- Mage-Captain Voss (wizard, flying)
- 4 Veteran Guards
- 2d4 Standard Guards
- 1d4 Guard Dogs

## THE TWIST REVEAL

### At the Mill (Handoff Location)
**What Happens:**
1. Wicket arrives with payment chest
2. Ravens reveals the truth
3. Wicket hired her to kill his family
4. He wanted insurance money (50,000 gp)
5. Never paid her the second half (5,000 gp)
6. Tipped off guards to have her arrested
7. This breakout is his way to pay her

### The Party's Choices

**Option 1: Complete the Job**
- Let Ravens take payment and leave
- Professional, not their problem
- Keep their payment (3,500 gp total)

**Option 2: Turn Them Both In**
- Report to city watch
- Justice is served
- Lose payment, make enemy of Klaws?

**Option 3: Kill Wicket**
- Vigilante justice
- Take his payment (5,000 gp)
- Ravens escapes
- Total: 7,500 gp

**Option 4: Kill Ravens**
- Wicket offers extra 5,000 gp
- Become accomplice to murder
- Very profitable (12,500 gp)
- Very dark

**Option 5: Kill Both**
- Take all money (10,000+ gp)
- No witnesses
- Extremely evil choice

**Option 6: Creative Solution**
- Blackmail Wicket ongoing
- Fake Ravens' death
- Recruit Ravens for Klaws
- Be clever!

## LOCKDOWN SEQUENCE

### When Alarm Triggers

**Round 1:**
- Bells ring, guards mobilize
- Closest patrol arrives in 2 rounds

**Round 2:**
- First patrol arrives (1d4+2 guards)
- Doors start locking (DC 20 to force)

**Round 3:**
- Second patrol (1d4+2 guards + veteran)
- Guard dogs released (2d4 dogs)

**Round 5:**
- Warden arrives (with 4 veterans)
- Mage-Captain arrives (flying)
- All exits sealed

**Every 2 Rounds After:**
- More reinforcements (1d6 guards)

---

## ESCAPE ROUTES

**Route 1: The Way In**
- Retrace infiltration path
- More guards now (increased patrols)

**Route 2: The Docks**
- Steal supply boat
- DC 12 to control boat in swift river
- Guards may pursue

**Route 3: Over the Walls**
- 30-foot climb (DC 15)
- Watchtowers shoot at you
- 30-foot drop into water on other side

**Route 4: Secret Tunnel**
- Basement maintenance area
- Requires clearing rubble (10 minutes)
- Emerges in abandoned warehouse

**Route 5: Fight Through**
- Boss battle in courtyard
- Extremely difficult
- Can signal Thokk for backup (costs favor)

## RED FLAGS ABOUT WICKET

**Insight Checks:**
- **DC 14:** He's hiding something, seems distressed
- **DC 18:** Distress is fear/guilt, not grief

**Observation:**
- Expensive mourning clothes (new, for show)
- Refers to family formally, no happy memories
- Nervously checks pocket watch (wife's)
- Never mentions family except deaths
