## FROM THE DESK OF OLP ZIX ZAP
*Kobold Klaws Interplanar Syndicate*

My dear associates,

Congratulations on making it this far! You've expressed interest in joining our... *prestigious* organization. Before we make things official, I require proof of your capabilities.

Tonight, you will undertake a test of skill, cunning, nerve, and more than likely danger.

---

## THE JOB

**TARGET:** The Shard of Eternal Binding
- A crystalline artifact of considerable value
- Currently held at the Twilight Vault auction house
- Will be auctioned at 9:00 PM sharp

**OBJECTIVE:** Acquire the Shard and deliver it to me
- Time limit: 3 hours (starting 6:00 PM)
- Method: Your choice (though discretion is advised)
- Deadline: Before the auction concludes

**COMPENSATION:**
- 1,000 gold pieces each
- A magical Item of your choice of the sydicates armory
- Full membership in the Kobold Klaws
- Access to our... *extensive* network of contacts

---

## WHAT I'M PROVIDING

**Disguises:**
- Three uniforms for Twilight Vault catering staff
- Name badges (enchanted to pass cursory inspection)
- Service entrance access credentials

**Equipment:**
- One Bag of Holding
- One Sending Stone (linked to me for emergencies)

**Intelligence:**
- Partial floor plan of the Twilight Vault (see attached)
- Guard rotation schedule (every 30 minutes)
- Notable attendees list

---

## WHAT I KNOW ABOUT THE TWILIGHT VAULT

**Security Measures:**
- Head of Security: Vexxus (a cambion, quite thorough)
- Guard Force: Veterans, well-trained and alert
- Additional Security: Magical wards on the vault
- The vault itself is in the basement level

**The Shard's Location:**
- Being held in the secure vault until auction time
- High-value storage area, maximum security
- Force field protection on display case

**Useful Information:**
- The auction attracts wealthy clientele from across the planes
- Staff are expected to be discrete and professional
- Security is tight, but staff areas have lighter coverage
- The kitchen connects to the basement levels

## KNOWN ATTENDEES TONIGHT

**High-Value Guests:**
- Several devil merchants from the Nine Hells
- Celestial collectors (at least two confirmed)
- Wealthy genasi nobility
- Mortal wizards of considerable power

**Staff:**
- Approximately 15 catering staff
- 6-8 security guards on rotation
- Vexxus (Head of Security) - rarely leaves his post
- Auction house manager (name unknown)

---

## RULES OF ENGAGEMENT

1. **Discretion is valuable.** The less attention you draw, the better.

2. **Violence is expensive.** Bodies require cleanup, and cleanup is costly. Avoid unnecessary casualties.

3. **Failure is... disappointing.** I appreciate effort, but results matter more.

4. **The Shard must be intact.** Damaged goods are worthless goods.

5. **You have one sending stone.** Use it wisely. I may have... adjustments to the plan.

---

## EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

**If Discovered:**
- Make your way to the rooftop
- I will arrange an emergency extraction point
- Do NOT attempt to flee through main entrance (too many witnesses)

**If Separated:**
- Regroup at the kitchen service entrance
- If that's compromised, rooftop is the fallback

**If Captured:**
- Kobold Klaws does not exist (officially)
- You're freelancers with a gambling debt
- I will... arrange alternatives, but don't expect me to storm the castle

---

## FINAL THOUGHTS

This is your audition. Show me you're clever, capable, and cool under pressure. The Kobold Klaws values professionals who can think on their feet and adapt to changing circumstances.

Style matters. Anyone can bash down a door. It takes true artistry to walk through it without anyone noticing.

The Twilight Vault has never been successfully robbed. Make history tonight.

Do try not to die. Good help is so hard to find.

Best of luck,

**Olp Zix Zap**
*Proprietor, Kobold Klaws Interplanar Syndicate*
