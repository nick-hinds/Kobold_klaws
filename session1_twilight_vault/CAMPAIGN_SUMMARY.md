# The Kobold Klaws Heist - Brief Summary

## Elevator Pitch
A stylish Ocean's Eleven-inspired D&D 5e heist one-shot where three level 6 characters must steal a magical artifact from a high-security auction house to earn membership in an interplanar crime syndicate run by a sophisticated kobold named Olp Zix Zap.

---

## The Setup
**Mission:** Steal the Shard of Eternal Binding from the Twilight Vault
**Employer:** Olp Zix Zap, dapper kobold crime boss of the Kobold Klaws
**Time Limit:** 3 hours before the Shard is auctioned and lost forever
**Stakes:** Prove yourselves worthy of joining the multiverse's most stylish criminal organization

---

## Three-Phase Heist

### Phase 1: Infiltration (Social/Stealth)
- Disguise as catering staff at a lavish auction
- Gather intel from drunk nobles and security schedules
- Optionally raid security office for vault control amulet
- Avoid the paranoid cambion security chief, Vexxus

### Phase 2: The Vault (Puzzles/Traps)
- Bypass a glowing purple arcane lock
- Navigate deadly pressure plate hallway
- Deal with a Shield Guardian (fight or control it)
- Disable magical force field around the Shard
- **ALARM!** Now you have 1 minute to escape

### Phase 3: Rooftop Escape (Combat/Action)
- Chase sequence through panicking crowds
- Defend rooftop position for 3 rounds
- Fight waves of guards and hell hounds
- Face off against flying Vexxus
- Dive through Olp's emergency portal to freedom

---

## What Makes It Special

**Style Over Violence:** Rewards clever planning and stealth over combat
**Memorable NPCs:** Olp Zix Zap is quotable and charming
**Flexible Gameplay:** Multiple solutions to every obstacle
**Campaign Ready:** Can launch ongoing Kobold Klaws adventures
**Complete Package:** Maps, stat blocks, handouts—everything you need

---

## Key Rewards
- 1,000 gp each
- Uncommon magic item of choice
- Kobold Klaw Pin (grants underworld contacts)
- Membership in interplanar crime syndicate
- Access to future heist jobs

---

## Perfect For
- DMs who want a change from dungeon crawls
- Players who enjoy planning and clever solutions
- Groups looking for a one-shot that could become more
- Fans of heist movies and crime capers
- Tables that enjoy memorable NPCs and roleplay

## Runtime
**2-3 hours** (perfect for a single session)
