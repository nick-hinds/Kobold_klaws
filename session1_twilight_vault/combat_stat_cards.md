## CONDITION TRACKING

**Common Conditions in This Adventure:**

**Charmed** (Vexxus's Fiendish Charm):
- Can't attack charmer
- Charmer has advantage on social checks
- Duration: 1 day (or until damage taken)

**Grappled** (Shield Guardian):
- Speed = 0
- Disadvantage on attacks against others
- Escape: DC 17 Athletics or Acrobatics

**Prone** (from knockback/trips):
- Disadvantage on attack rolls
- Attacks against have advantage (melee)
- Half movement to stand up

**Frightened** (situational):
- Disadvantage on checks/attacks
- Can't move toward source of fear
