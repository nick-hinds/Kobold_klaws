## PHASE 1: INFILTRATION ENCOUNTERS

### Social Encounter: Suspicious Vexxus
If the party draws too much attention, Vexxus approaches to investigate.

**Skill Challenge (3 successes before 2 failures):**
- **Deception DC 14:** Maintain cover story
- **Performance DC 13:** Act like competent staff
- **Persuasion DC 15:** Convince him you belong here
- **Insight DC 16:** Read his suspicions and adjust approach

**Success:** He's satisfied and leaves them with a warning
**Failure:** He assigns a guard to "help" them (follows them around, disadvantage on Stealth)

## ROOFTOP FINALE - Wave Defense

**Setup:** Party must hold position for 3 rounds while portal stabilizes. Enemies appear in waves.

**Wave 1 (Round 1):**
- 2 Veteran Guards (climbing up from below)
- 1 Hell Hound (leaping onto roof)

**Wave 2 (Round 2):**
- 2 More Veteran Guards
- 1 More Hell Hound
- Vexxus arrives (flying)

**Wave 3 (Round 3):**
- Vexxus focuses attacks
- Any remaining guards press forward
- **Portal activates at END of Round 3** - party can escape

**Victory Conditions:**
- Survive 3 rounds OR
- Reduce Vexxus to 25 HP (he calls retreat) OR
- Defeat all enemies

**Portal:** Glowing purple swirling gateway. Diving through it requires 10 ft. of movement and ends the encounter.

## SKILL CHALLENGE: THE CHASE SEQUENCE

**Goal:** Escape the building and reach the rooftop
**Required:** 6 successes before 3 failures
**Time Pressure:** Each round of chase, enemies get closer

**Available Skills & DCs:**

**Athletics DC 14:** Parkour over obstacles, climb stairs quickly
**Acrobatics DC 13:** Dodge through crowds, slide under closing doors
**Stealth DC 15:** Hide in side rooms, lose pursuers in confusion
**Deception DC 14:** Blend into panicking auction guests
**Persuasion DC 16:** Convince guests to block pursuers
**Arcana DC 13:** Magically seal doors behind you
**Perception DC 12:** Spot shortcuts and alternate routes

**Creative Solutions:**
- Illusion magic to create false paths
- Dimension Door / Misty Step to skip obstacles (automatic success)
- Clever use of environment (flipping tables, breaking chandeliers)

**Success:** Reach rooftop with 1 round head start before enemies arrive
**Failure:** Enemies arrive immediately when portal is still unstabilized (4 rounds to hold instead of 3)

## NOTES FOR DM

**Scaling Difficulty:**
- **Easier:** Remove 1 hell hound, give party +1 minor healing potion each
- **Harder:** Add animated armor in vault, Vexxus appears in round 1 of rooftop

**Roleplay Opportunities:**
- Olp is extremely quotable - give him personality!
- Auction guests can be colorful NPCs (devil merchants, celestial collectors, eccentric wizards)
- Vexxus respects professionalism - party can earn his grudging respect

**Time Management:**
- Phase 1 (Infiltration): 30-45 minutes
- Phase 2 (Vault): 45-60 minutes  
- Phase 3 (Escape): 45-60 minutes
- Total: 2-3 hours including roleplay
