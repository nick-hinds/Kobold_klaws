# KOBOLD KLAWS HEIST - QUICK REFERENCE SHEET

## MISSION BRIEFING
**Job:** Steal the Shard of Eternal Binding from Twilight Vault
**Time Limit:** 3 hours before item is auctioned
**Reward:** 1,000 gp each + uncommon magic item + Kobold Klaws membership
**Contact:** Olp Zix Zap (sophisticated kobold crime boss)

## QUICK DCs

### Social/Stealth
- Blend in as staff: **DC 12-14**
- Befriend drunk NPC: **DC 12**
- Avoid Vexxus's suspicion: **DC 14-16**
- Move quietly in vault: **DC 10-13**

### Vault Obstacles  
- Detect pressure plates: **Perception DC 14**
- Disarm pressure plates: **Thieves' Tools DC 15**
- Arcane door dispel: **Arcana DC 16**
- Disable force field: **Arcana DC 17 or Dispel Magic**
- Security office lock: **Thieves' Tools DC 14**

### Combat
- Shadows: AC 12, HP 16, Weakness: Radiant
- Shield Guardian: AC 17, HP 142, Regenerates 10/round
- Veterans: AC 17, HP 58 each
- Hell Hounds: AC 15, HP 45, Fire Breath (Recharge 5-6)
- Vexxus: AC 19, HP 82, Flies, Fire Ray +6 (3d10), Retreats at 25 HP

## PHASE BREAKDOWN

### PHASE 1: INFILTRATION (30-45 min)
**Goal:** Get to vault entrance
- Party enters as catering staff
- Gather intel from drunk fire genasi (Kaz)
- Optional: Break into security office for amulet
- Kitchen entrance at southwest leads to vault

### PHASE 2: THE VAULT (45-60 min)
**Goal:** Steal the Shard
1. Bypass arcane door (password or Arcana check)
2. Navigate pressure plates (detect & disarm or bypass)
3. Deal with Shield Guardian (fight or use amulet)
4. Open force field case (Dispel or Arcana)
5. Grab Shard - **ALARM TRIGGERS - 1 MIN TO ESCAPE!**

### PHASE 3: ESCAPE (45-60 min)
**Goal:** Reach rooftop portal
- Chase skill challenge (6 successes before 3 failures)
- Rooftop defense for 3 rounds
- Portal opens end of Round 3
- Dive through to escape!

## COMMON PLAYER TRICKS

**"Can we use Disguise Self?"** - Yes! Makes Phase 1 easier.

**"I cast Invisibility and grab it."** - Smart! But force field still needs disabled.

**"We kill all the guards."** - Possible but alerts everyone. Stealth fails.

**"Can we negotiate with Vexxus?"** - He's professional. Might accept bribe but it'd be EXPENSIVE.

**"What if we destroy the Shard?"** - Olp is NOT happy. No payment.
