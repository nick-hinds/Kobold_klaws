## ROLL20 SETUP INSTRUCTIONS

### Uploading Maps
1. Go to your Roll20 game
2. Click "Map" layer
3. Upload each PNG file as a new map page
4. **CRITICAL:** Set grid alignment:
   - Right-click map ‚Üí "Align to Grid"
   - Set grid size to **70 pixels**
   - Each square = 5 feet
   - Make sure corners align perfectly

### Dynamic Lighting Setup (Optional)
For each map, set up walls using the Dynamic Lighting tool:
- **Walls:** Draw along all gray/dark walls
- **Doors:** Leave gaps for doors (can be closed/opened)
- **Vision blocking:** Use for security office and vault areas

### Token Recommendations
- **Players:** Standard player tokens
- **Olp Zix Zap:** Small-sized token (kobold in suit)
- **Vexxus:** Medium token with wings
- **Guards:** Medium humanoid tokens
- **Hell Hounds:** Medium beast tokens  
- **Shield Guardian:** Large construct token
- **Shadows:** Medium undead tokens (translucent)

**A. Auction Stage (North, Squares 6-24, Rows 1-3)**

**B. Seating Area (Center, Rows 5-15)**

- Vexxus's command center
- Door at Square 22, Row 4

**E. Guard Posts (Red Circles)**
- Square 27, Row 10 (East guard)
- Square 2, Row 10 (West guard)
- Veterans on patrol, rotate every 30 min
- **Tactics:** Guards watch for suspicious behavior

**F. Main Entrance (South, Squares 14-16, Row 24)**
- Guests enter here
- Invitation check by guards
- **Note:** Party enters through service/staff entrance

- **6:00 PM:** Auction begins, party arrives
- **6:30 PM:** First guard rotation
- **7:00 PM:** High-value items start (Shard auction at 9 PM)

## MAP 2: VAULT BASEMENT
**Grid Size:** 25 x 20 squares (125 ft x 100 ft)
**File:** map_2_vault_basement.png

### Key Locations

**A. Entrance Stairs (North, Squares 11-14, Rows 0-3)**
- Stone stairs from kitchen
- Door at top (not shown, in kitchen)
- 15 ft descent
- **Stealth:** DC 12 to descend quietly

**B. Arcane Lock Corridor (Squares 11-14, Rows 3-8)**
- Glowing purple door (magical barrier)
- **Door:** Purple rectangle at Row 3
- **Solutions:**
  - Cast Knock (automatic)
  - Arcana check DC 16 (10 min to dispel)
  - Password from Vexxus's office: "Mammon's Profit"
  - Brute force: DC 20 Athletics (triggers alarm)

**C. Pressure Plate Hallway (Squares 2-23, Rows 8-14)**
- 5 red-marked pressure plates (!):
  - Square 8, Row 9
  - Square 12, Row 10
  - Square 6, Row 11
  - Square 16, Row 12
  - Square 10, Row 13
- **Detection:** Perception DC 14
- **Disarm:** Thieves' Tools DC 15 each
- **Trigger:** Summons 3 Shadows (appear from walls)
- **Bypass:** Fly, Misty Step, careful movement

**D. Guardian Chamber (Squares 2-23, Rows 14-18)**
- Shield Guardian at center (blue/purple circle)
- Positioned at Squares 11.5-13.5, Rows 15.5-17.5
- **Guardian:** 142 HP, AC 17, regenerates 10 HP/round
- **Control Amulet:** In Vexxus's office desk
- **Combat:** Will grapple intruders, very tough fight
- **Bypass:** Show amulet = guardian steps aside peacefully

**E. Vault Room (Squares 8-17, Rows 17-19)**
- Golden floor indicates treasure vault
- High security area
- Force field display case at center

**F. The Shard Display (Squares 11.5-13.5, Rows 17.5-18.5)**
- Glowing blue force cage
- Golden crystal visible inside (the Shard!)
- **Force Field:** 
  - Dispel Magic (automatic)
  - Arcana check DC 17 to disable
  - Damage it: AC 20, 50 HP, immune to physical
- **Alarm:** Triggers when opened, 1 min until guards arrive

### Trap Details

**Pressure Plates:**
- Hidden unless detected
- Each is 5x5 ft square
- Weighted (250+ lbs triggers)
- Can disable individually
- All connected to same alarm system

**Shadow Encounter (If triggered):**
- 3 Shadows emerge from walls
- Surprise round if party didn't detect plates
- Shadows use hit-and-run tactics
- Vulnerable to radiant damage
- Combat music: intense chase theme

### Stealth Challenge
Party should stay quiet throughout vault:
- **DC 10 Stealth:** Moving normally
- **DC 13 Stealth:** Opening doors, disarming traps
- **DC 15 Stealth:** Combat (if fighting guardian/shadows)
- **Failure:** Guards upstairs hear (arrive in 3 rounds)

## MAP 3: ROOFTOP FINALE
**Grid Size:** 28 x 22 squares (140 ft x 110 ft)
**File:** map_3_rooftop_finale.png

### Key Locations

**A. Portal Zone (Center-North, Squares 11-16, Rows 8-12)**
- Purple concentric circles = portal location
- **Round 1:** Portal unstable, can't use
- **Round 2:** Portal stabilizing, flickering
- **Round 3:** Portal fully open - party can escape!
- Escape requires 10 ft movement to dive through
- **Note:** Portal is 20 ft wide (4 squares)

**B. Access Stairs (South, Squares 12-16, Rows 19-21)**
- Only way up from building
- Red squares = enemy spawn points
- Enemies emerge here each wave
- Party can bottleneck here defensively

**C. Cover Positions (Multiple locations)**
- **Crates (NW):** Squares 5-7, Rows 5-7 - Full cover
- **HVAC Unit (NE):** Squares 21-23, Rows 5-7 - Full cover
- **Vents (SW):** Squares 5-7, Rows 15-17 - Half cover
- **Cover (SE):** Squares 21-23, Rows 15-17 - Half cover
- **Crates (S):** Squares 13-15, Row 15 - Half cover

**D. Open Sky (Entire center area)**
- Vexxus can fly freely here
- No ceiling, open air
- High winds: DC 10 Acrobatics or fall prone (optional)
- 40 ft drop to next building (4d6 damage)

**E. Roof Edges (All outer walls)**
- Low walls (3 ft high) = half cover
- Can climb over (10 ft movement)
- Not recommended escape route!

### Wave-by-Wave Encounter

**ROUND 1:**
- **Enemies:** 2 Veterans, 1 Hell Hound
- **Spawn:** Veterans at stairs, Hell Hound leaps up from edge
- **Tactics:** Veterans advance with crossbows, Hound flanks
- **Portal Status:** Swirling, unstable

**ROUND 2:**
- **New Enemies:** 2 More Veterans, 1 Hell Hound, VEXXUS arrives
- **Spawn:** Vexxus flies in from south (off-map)
- **Tactics:** Vexxus stays airborne using Fire Ray, directs guards
- **Portal Status:** Flickering, taking shape

**ROUND 3:**
- **All Remaining Enemies press attack**
- **Vexxus:** Focuses fire on lowest HP character
- **Portal Status:** FULLY OPEN - escape available!
- **Victory:** Survive to end of round OR reduce Vexxus to 25 HP

### Tactical Considerations

**For Players:**
- **Defensive:** Hold position near portal using cover
- **Aggressive:** Push toward stairs to limit spawns
- **Divide & Conquer:** Split focus (melee on hounds, ranged on Vexxus)
- **Escape:** Portal opens end of Round 3, dive through!

**For DM:**
- Vexxus should feel threatening but not impossible
- If party is struggling, have Vexxus focus on taunting/monologuing
- If party is dominating, have more guards climb up
- Hell Hounds should use Fire Breath to control space
- Vexxus retreats at 25 HP: "You'll regret this!"

### Environmental Hazards (Optional)
- **Strong Winds:** Every round, DC 10 Acrobatics or prone
- **Debris:** Scattered on roof, difficult terrain
- **Ledges:** Falling = 4d6 damage, lose 1 round climbing back

## MAP 4: SECURITY OFFICE
**Grid Size:** 12 x 12 squares (60 ft x 60 ft)
**File:** map_4_security_office.png

### Key Locations

**A. Entrance Door (West, Square 0.5, Row 6)**
- Only entrance/exit
- Usually locked (DC 14 to pick)
- Vexxus has key
- **Alarm:** If forced open (Strength DC 20), alerts Vexxus

**B. Vexxus's Desk (Northeast, Squares 7-10, Rows 2-3.5)**
- Large wooden desk
- **Golden circle on desk = Control Amulet location**
- Locked drawer: DC 14 Thieves' Tools
- **Loot:**
  - Shield Guardian control amulet
  - Password note: "Mammon's Profit" (vault door)
  - Schedule of guard rotations
  - 200 gp in petty cash

**C. Filing Cabinets (West, Squares 2-4, Rows 2-5)**
- Two filing cabinets
- Contain security records, guest lists
- Investigation DC 12: Find information about other auction items
- **Plot Hooks:** Future heist targets!

**D. Security Monitors (East, Squares 7-10, Rows 5-7)**
- Six monitor screens showing:
  - Vault entrance
  - Pressure plate hallway
  - Guardian chamber
  - Auction floor (2 angles)
  - Rooftop
- **Note:** Guards watch these - stealth matters!

**E. Safe (Southwest, Squares 2-3.5, Rows 8-9.5)**
- Heavy steel safe (gray square)
- Locked: DC 18 Thieves' Tools or Knock spell
- **Contains:**
  - 100 gp
  - 2 potions of greater healing
  - 1 potion of invisibility
  - Blackmail documents (valuable to Olp!)

**F. Guard Chair (Center, Square 9-10, Row 6-7)**
- Where guard sits when monitoring
- Usually empty (guard on patrol)
- Coffee mug, half-eaten sandwich

### Infiltration Options

**Stealth Entry:**
- Pick lock: Thieves' Tools DC 14
- Wait for guard patrol (30 min cycle)
- **Stealth DC 13** to enter and search quietly

**Disguise Entry:**
- Pretend to be cleaning staff
- Deception DC 15 if guard present
- Performance DC 12 to act naturally

**Magic Entry:**
- Knock spell on door (alerts guards via sound)
- Invisibility (DC 10 Stealth to avoid bumping things)
- Misty Step through door

**Combat Entry (Not Recommended):**
- Alerts entire facility
- Reinforcements in 2 rounds
- Ruins heist attempt

### Search Times
- **Quick search (1 minute):** Find obvious items (amulet, cash)
- **Thorough search (10 minutes):** Find all loot and secrets
- **Safe cracking (5 minutes):** Additional time if picking lock

### What Vexxus Knows
If interrogated or documents found:
- Vault password: "Mammon's Profit"
- Guardian respects the control amulet bearer
- Pressure plates trigger at 250 lbs+
- Shard is worth 10,000+ gp to collectors
- Next big auction item: Ancient Lich's phylactery (future hook!)

## ENCOUNTER DIFFICULTY SCALING

### If Party is Struggling:

**Phase 1 (Infiltration):**
- Vexxus is distracted, won't confront them
- Give them Kaz encounter automatically
- Hints about office having useful info

**Phase 2 (Vault):**
- One pressure plate is already disabled
- Guardian has only 100 HP
- Alarm takes 2 minutes instead of 1

**Phase 3 (Escape):**
- Remove 1 Hell Hound
- Vexxus arrives Round 3 instead of Round 2
- Portal opens end of Round 2

### If Party is Dominating:

**Phase 1:**
- Vexxus personally checks them out
- More guards on patrol
- Kitchen staff questions their presence

**Phase 2:**
- All pressure plates are active
- Guardian has max HP + resistance to magic
- Shadows appear even if plates avoided (spectral alarm)

**Phase 3:**
- Add 2 more guards
- Vexxus uses Fiendish Charm aggressively
- Portal takes until Round 4
- Hell Hounds coordinate Fire Breath

- Sophisticated despite being a kobold
- Always has a backup plan

## TROUBLESHOOTING

**Problem:** Party gets caught in Phase 1
**Solution:** Social encounter, chase back to kitchen, alarm raised early

**Problem:** Party can't solve arcane door
**Solution:** Kaz drunkenly mentions seeing "Vexxus type something"

**Problem:** TPK possible in Phase 2
**Solution:** Guardian just restrains them, guards arrive, arrested not killed

**Problem:** Rooftop too easy/hard
**Solution:** Adjust waves, have Vexxus flee earlier/later

**Problem:** Party tries completely different approach
**Solution:** Reward creativity! Olp loves style. Adjust as needed.

## FINAL NOTES

This is a **heist one-shot** - it's meant to be fun, fast-paced, and stylish. Don't sweat perfect balance. If players come up with a clever plan you didn't anticipate, let them try it! The goal is Ocean's Eleven meets D&D, not Dark Souls difficulty.

Olp Zix Zap should become a memorable NPC. Ham it up! Make him quotable.

Remember: The Kobold Klaws are *professional criminals*, not murder hobos. Stealth and style over combat.
